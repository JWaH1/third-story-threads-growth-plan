2026 Pantograph Guide

Curated recommendations for modern quilts. Pair these with current fabric trends for maximum impact.

Geometric Bold
Best for: Tula Pink, large-scale modern prints
• Linear & angular designs that let prints shine
• Avoid dense fills on bold graphics
Organic Texture
Best for: Earthy 2026 palettes, solids, low-volume
• Meandering, woodgrain, pebble textures
• Adds depth without competing
Floral & Curved
Best for: Heather Ross, delicate florals
• Looser, flowing pantographs
• Echo the scale of the print
